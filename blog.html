Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask & Machine Learning

In the digital health era, we developed a user-centric, symptom-based disease diagnosis web application using Flask and a Decision Tree Classifier. This platform is designed to empower users with instant insights based on reported symptoms.

The Problem

Health concerns often arise from symptoms we don’t fully understand. Our goal was to bridge that knowledge gap and provide quick, informed predictions when symptoms appear—especially where access to doctors is limited or delayed.

The Solution

Our app works through a simple yet powerful workflow:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms via a streamlined interface.
  2. ML Model: A trained Decision Tree Classifier maps these symptoms to potential diseases.
  3. Prediction: The model returns a likely diagnosis in real-time.
  4. Insights: Users receive disease descriptions, medications, diet tips, and more.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean and accessible UI for all age groups.
  • Accurate Predictions: Machine learning-backed insights improve reliability.
  • Comprehensive Information: Beyond predictions, users get helpful resources.
  • Educational Blogs: Informative posts to enhance health awareness and literacy.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis App is more than just a tool—it's a step toward informed, accessible digital healthcare. By blending AI with medical data, we aim to empower users and improve public health awareness worldwide.
